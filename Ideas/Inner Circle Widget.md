# Inner Circle Widget (Shelved)

> **Status:** Shelved until financial stability achieved ($5K/month)

---

## Core Idea

An iPhone widget app for your inner circle of friends where friends can easily change their status — a simple way to check in on how your close people are doing.

---

## What It's NOT

- Not a social media app
- Not for broad networks
- Not for content sharing

---

## What It IS

- A tight group that wants to make sure everyone is physically and mentally healthy
- Everyone knows when to check in
- Everyone knows when someone needs a friend
- Easy status updates (not posts, just states)

---

## Origin

Filmed 3 episodes of a show called "Inner Circle" with friends:
- Discussed current topics
- What we learned
- What we want to learn
- Never released

This widget idea is an evolution of that concept — making the inner circle accessible and actionable daily.

---

## Why It's Shelved

- Doesn't directly generate income
- Would require app development
- Consumer app = hard monetization
- Need to stabilize finances first
- Revisit when at $5K/month consistent
