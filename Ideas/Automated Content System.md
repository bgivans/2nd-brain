# Automated Content System

> February 8, 2025. Early ideation on building a content automation system — for GG clients or as a standalone product. Sits at the intersection of the social media/content work and the systems-building instinct.

---

## The Concept

An automated content creation pipeline that takes inputs (brand voice, offer, target audience) and produces ready-to-post content at scale. The goal: eliminate the manual bottleneck in content creation for small businesses that need consistent output but don't have a content team.

The automation layers being considered:
1. **Input:** client brief, brand guidelines, content pillars
2. **Generation:** AI drafts (captions, scripts, carousel copy, hooks)
3. **Formatting:** output structured by platform (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn)
4. **Scheduling:** integration with a posting tool (Later, Buffer, or GHL)
5. **Reporting:** performance data fed back to improve future content

---

## The GG Use Case

At the time of this session (February 2025), GG was already doing content work for clients — Javier's Unlock Your Property brand, Ellie's C2C Legal, Urban Management. The manual content creation process was a time constraint.

An automated system would:
- Let GG deliver more content per client with less per-piece time
- Create a repeatable process that scales
- Potentially become a standalone service ("Content System Install" at $2,000/build)

---

## The Product Angle

If built as a product rather than an internal tool:
- SaaS for small business owners who want consistent content without a content manager
- White-label version GG sells as a retainer add-on ($200-300/month)
- AI-generated but human-reviewed — the "done-for-you" content system

This is distinct from → [[Ideas/AI Content Creation SaaS]] which was more about a consumer tool. This version is the B2B operator system.
