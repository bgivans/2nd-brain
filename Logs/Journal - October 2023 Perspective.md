# Journal — October 2023: Perspective

> Voice-to-text journal entry. Cleaned up for readability but the ideas are preserved as written. This is Bryce before Givans Global existed — still embedded in the music world, already pulling away from it.

---

## The Honest Inventory

Surrounded by people of influence and quiet power in the music industry. Not satisfied. Not enjoying the time. It feels like what I *must* do — not what I truly want to do.

What I know I'm most interested in: **running an online business and scaling it through marketing and content.** I am passionate about marketing.

---

## What I Saw in the Music World

Early on I was in a studio in LA with Castelo. It was cool. You could feel the history. But it also felt like more play than work — more fun, more smoke, more drink — not focused on the actual music.

People he called brothers at the time — close to him in that world — had taken their creative talent and turned it into a career. Proud of them, but concerned they were no longer focused on the music. Chasing fame first, believing that once they achieved it, they'd have money, and could come back to making music freely with the most talented people in the world.

*(Note: "brothers" was the word used at the time. These relationships have since shifted — not people Bryce would describe that way now.)*

**The trap I observed:** To collaborate with the best, you need status. To build status, you optimize for image, presentation, and fame. That optimization slowly replaces the original goal. Fame is the prerequisite that eats the dream.

> *"I find fame such a toxic thing and I don't want any part."*

---

## What I Actually Want (As Clear As I Could Get)

> *"My ultimate passion is to create with purpose and leave an impact with marketing and content."*

A tool or new solution that the world needs — delivered through content and marketing. That's what I sync with.

I want:
- Freedom of time
- People I love around me
- Money — but with clear eyes: more money brings new expenses, new problems. The number keeps moving.
- Brielle in my life
- Family time

What I don't want: the fame trap. The image optimization. Being absorbed by how I look online instead of what I'm building.

---

## The Honest Confusion

I couldn't put into words what I was truly desiring. Just keep-on-keeping-on. Feeling low purpose, though small things gave glimpses of excitement.

> *"Is this my awakening or have I just been in a room with too much weed for too long?"*

The question was real: is this clarity or restlessness? Looking back — it was both. The restlessness was pointing somewhere true.

---

## The Closing Note

**Go all in. Don't half-ass anything.**

Written at the bottom of the entry, almost as a reminder to himself.
