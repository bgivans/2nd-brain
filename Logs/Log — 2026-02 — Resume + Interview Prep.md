## Key Interview Frameworks (from preparation)

**Behavioral questions (STAR method):**
- **Situation:** Set the context briefly
- **Task:** What were you responsible for?
- **Action:** What specifically did you do?
- **Result:** What was the measurable outcome?

**The "tell me about yourself" answer:**
Lead with the operator identity — not the resume chronology. *"I build systems that help businesses grow faster than they break. Most of my work is at the intersection of AI automation, operations, and marketing infrastructure..."*

**Questions to ask the interviewer:**
- What does success look like in the first 90 days?
- What are the biggest operational challenges this team is navigating?
- How does this team use technology to scale?
