# Ellie System Build Brainstorm

## Session Summary

Long brainstorm session covering Google Takeout as a second brain foundation, full audit of Ellie's landing page, ad strategy, lead gen channels, cost-per-lead analysis, and implementation roadmap. Ended with a key insight about phone vs. in-person close rate gap.

---

## Big Ideas Surfaced

### Google Takeout → Second Brain
- Use Google Takeout to import years of existing data (Gmail, Drive, Calendar) as the foundation of an Obsidian vault
- For Ellie specifically: not client data, but her **decision-making framework** — how she qualifies leads, handles case nuances, answers team questions
- Pulls from Microsoft ecosystem + Trello history too
- Three vault layers: Decision Library, Regulatory Feed, Training Codex
- This becomes a **team oracle** — case manager queries instead of interrupting Ellie

### Ellie Brand Architecture Decision
- Build around **Ellie Dominguez** (personal brand), not C2C Legal Services
- She's 63, wants to exit in ~2 years
- Business won't outlive her — but her **name and credibility will**
- C2C becomes a footnote in the footer, not the headline
- Personal brand equity travels with her into whatever's next

### Landing Page Audit
**What's working:**
- "Fast, Flat-Rate Document Preparation" — solid hero headline
- Bilingual angle (major differentiator in SD/Riverside)
- Testimonials with geo-specific attribution
- Two physical office addresses = credibility
- Payment flexibility section
- FAQ section is sharp and specific

**What needs fixing:**
- Floating avatar covers copy on every section — kill it
- LDA registration placeholders still showing `[LDA_REG_NUMBER]` etc. — fix today
- Form is buried at the bottom — needs to be above the fold
- "Free Consultation" CTA is weak — change to "Check If You Qualify" or "See If We Can Help"
- No price anchor anywhere — add at least one starting price
- Page is doing too much for ad traffic — needs separate `/lp/` version

### Page Strategy
- **Current page** → Keep as main brand/trust site for organic + referral traffic
- **Build `/lp/` versions** → One per service (divorce, bankruptcy, trusts)
  - No nav, no footer links, no email signup
  - Hero → proof → qualification form → single CTA
  - Ad traffic only
