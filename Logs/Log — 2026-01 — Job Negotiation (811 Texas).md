## The Negotiation Context

Key strategies from this session:

**Don't anchor low:** The first person to name a number often loses. If asked for salary expectations, respond with: *"I'm flexible, but I'd want to understand the full compensation package — base, equity, and benefits — before settling on a number."*

**Research the band:** Glassdoor, LinkedIn Salary, levels.fyi (for tech roles) give the actual range. Negotiate toward the top of the band, not the midpoint.

**Leverage:** The strongest negotiation leverage is another offer or a clear alternative. In this case, the alternative was GG — a real business with real clients, not just "I'm figuring something out."

**Benefits matter:** Health insurance, PTO, remote flexibility, equipment — these have dollar values. Factor them into the comparison.
