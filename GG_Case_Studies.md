## Case Study 1: C2C Legal — Ellie Dominguez

### Client
C2C Document Preparation — legal filing company serving 300+ active clients

### Problem
- No visibility on client pipeline — team couldn't see what step each client was at
- Internal confusion across operations
- No system for what needed to happen next

### What I Built
- CRM structure for tracking 300+ active clients with clear pipeline stages
- SOPs for team operations
- Microsoft Teams / Microsoft 365 organization and permissions
- Lead intake optimization
- Workflow for identifying inefficiencies

### Result
- Team can now clearly track and manage all clients
- Full visibility on every client's current step
- Clear understanding of next actions
- Reduced internal confusion

### Proof
- Blurred screenshot of CRM board available for confidential sharing
- Live site: https://elliedominguez-landing-page.lovable.app

### How to Use in a Pitch
> "I built a CRM structure for a legal services company with 300+ active clients. Before: the team had no visibility into who was where in the pipeline. After: every client is trackable, every step is clear, and the team can work without constant back-and-forth."

## Case Study 2: Unlock Your Property — Javier Feria

### Client
Unlock Your Property — real estate team focused on probate and distressed properties

### Problem
- Cold callers dialing blindly without context
- Thousands of potential leads with no organization or prioritization
- No system for knowing who was worth calling first

### What I Built
- Lead research and organization system
- Probate and distressed-property signal identification
- Targeted, usable lead lists with context on why each lead matters
- Workflow for lead enrichment and routing

### Result
- Cold callers have targeted lists instead of raw data
- Stronger context behind why each lead is worth contacting
- More efficient calling sessions

### Proof
- Demo available at https://givansglobal.com/ai-automation (Demo #2)
- Live site: https://unlock-so-cal-hero.lovable.app/
- Personal page: https://www.unlockyourproperty.com/bryce

### How to Use in a Pitch
> "I built a lead research system for a real estate team doing probate and distressed-property outreach. Before: cold callers were dialing blind. After: every call has context — who the lead is, why they might sell, and what the right approach is."

## Pitching These to New Clients

**The framing that works** (from Javier/Ellie relationship):

> "These aren't [your industry], but they're strong examples of the exact part I handle well: system-building, lead research, pipeline visibility, cleaner follow-up structure, and creating a better operational foundation so a team can work more efficiently and close more opportunities."

**The value isn't in the page or logo — it's in:**
- Looking more credible to prospects
- Going after more targeted opportunities
- Following up in a cleaner, more consistent way
- Reducing wasted time
- Creating a system you can keep using and improving
