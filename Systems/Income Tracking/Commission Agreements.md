## What Needs to Be Defined

Before building anything else, get **written agreements** that define:

1. **What counts as a "lead" or "client" you brought in**
   - Website form submission?
   - Ad click?
   - Specific UTM parameter?
   - Phone call from landing page?

2. **How attribution is tracked**
   - CRM tags?
   - Hidden form fields?
   - Manual review?

3. **Commission percentage**
   - 5%? 10%? Flat fee per lead?

4. **Payment timing**
   - Monthly?
   - Upon close?
   - Net 30?

5. **Duration**
   - 6 months?
   - 12 months?
   - Ongoing until cancelled?
