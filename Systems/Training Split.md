# Training Split

> Built from a May 2025 workout design session (9 messages) + a January 2026 shoulder workout session. The gap in [[Beliefs/Health Goals]] was that goals existed without a system. This is the system.

---

## Current Split Structure

Based on the sessions, the split being designed was a **Push / Pull / Legs** or similar upper/lower structure — focused on physique goals (body composition) rather than athletic performance.

**Target:** Cut body fat while maintaining or building muscle mass. The body composition sessions (→ [[Beliefs/Health Goals]]) gave the goal; this split is the execution.

---

## Pull Day

Designed for back and bicep development:

**Primary:**
- Pull-ups / Lat Pulldowns — 4 × 8-10 (lat width)
- Barbell or Dumbbell Rows — 3 × 10-12 (lat thickness)
- Cable Rows or Machine Rows — 3 × 12 (mid-back)

**Secondary:**
- Face Pulls — 3 × 15 (rear delt, shoulder health)
- Dumbbell Curls — 3 × 12
- Hammer Curls — 2 × 15

**Key principle:** Full range of motion over heavy weight. The pull is the starting point — strength follows form.

---

## Shoulder Day (January 2026 Killer Shoulder Workout)

A dedicated shoulder session was added in January 2026 — suggests the split evolved to include an isolated shoulder day, or shoulders are trained as part of push day with this as the structure:

**Primary:**
- Overhead Press (barbell or dumbbell) — 4 × 6-8 (strength foundation)
- Lateral Raises — 4 × 12-15 (side delt width)
- Front Raises or Arnold Press — 3 × 12 (front delt)

**Secondary:**
- Rear Delt Flies — 3 × 15 (rear delt balance)
- Cable Lateral Raises — 2 × 15 (constant tension)
- Shrugs — 3 × 12 (trap/shoulder connection)

**The "killer" framing:** High volume, moderate weight, shorter rest periods. More pump, more metabolic stress — leans toward hypertrophy + conditioning rather than max strength.

## The Full Week

| Day | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| Day 1 | Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) |
| Day 2 | Pull (Back, Biceps) |
| Day 3 | Legs |
| Day 4 | Rest or Active Recovery |
| Day 5 | Push (Strength emphasis) |
| Day 6 | Pull / Shoulders (Volume emphasis) |
| Day 7 | Rest |

Adjust based on schedule. The split can compress to 4 days if the week doesn't allow 6.

## Nutrition Alignment

Post-workout nutrition session (January 2026) pointed toward:
- Protein within 30-60 min post-workout (goal: 0.8-1g per lb bodyweight daily)
- Carbs post-workout to replenish glycogen
- Caloric deficit for cut — not aggressive, ~300-500 below maintenance
