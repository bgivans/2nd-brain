## Seller Script Ranking System

### Ranking 99–100 — High Net Worth Sellers

**Hook options:**
- "Ready to take your investments to the next level? Your property could be the key."
- "You've built your wealth — now let your home work for you."

**Script A:**
> "Do you own a property and feel ready to take the next big leap in your life? Imagine turning your home into the investment that sets your dreams in motion. If you've grown your net worth in the past five years and are ready to sell, we're here to help you sell quickly and efficiently. You can use the value of your property to invest in the next big opportunity — whether it's buying a business or growing your wealth. No obligations, just possibilities. Click the link to see how we can help."

### Ranking 95–99 — Urgent Need to Sell

**Hook options:**
- "Need to sell fast? We can close in 72 hours."
- "Facing foreclosure? Sell quickly and stress-free."

**Script A:**
> "Do you need to sell your property quickly? Whether it's probate, foreclosure, back taxes, or liquidation of a trust — we can help you sell both quickly and efficiently, on market or off. Don't let financial stress hold you back. Fill out the form and we'll get back to you within hours."

### Ranking 90–95 — Cash Offer Needed Now

**Hook options:**
- "Need cash for your home fast? Get an offer within 24 hours."
- "No commissions, no hassle — just a fair price."

**Script A:**
> "Looking for a fast cash offer? No commissions, no hassle — just a fair price when you need it most. Whether you're facing foreclosure or simply need to sell fast, we're here. Fill out the form and get a cash offer today."

### Ranking 80–90 — Fair Market Valuation Sellers

**Hook options:**
- "Ready for a fresh start? Move inland and get more for your money."
- "Want to relocate for better value? We can help you sell at fair market price."

**Script A:**
> "Thinking of selling to move further inland for more value? We offer fair market valuations to help you sell quickly so you can get the most out of your current property. Let us make this transition smooth. Click the link to get started."

### Ranking 70–80 — Financial Struggles

**Hook options:**
- "Struggling with debt? We can help you sell your home quickly and start fresh."
- "High debt holding you back? Find relief by selling your home."

**Script A:**
> "Are financial difficulties making it hard to hold onto your home? If high revolving debt is holding you back, we offer solutions — starting with selling your home quickly. Don't let debt dictate your future. Fill out the form and let us help you take the first step toward financial relief."
