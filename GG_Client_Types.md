# GIVANS GLOBAL — CLIENT TYPE GLOSSARY

## Real Estate Agent
Who: Individual licensed agents, not brokerages
Primary CTA: Book a call / Schedule a consultation
Audience device: Mobile-first (agents and clients both on phone)
Trust signals: License number, years in market, homes sold count, 
local area name in headline
Font: Inter + Playfair Display
Design archetype: Premium & Sleek
Hero headline formula: "[X] homes sold in [City] — yours could be next"
Tone: Professional, confident, locally anchored
Tone to avoid: Corporate, cold, aggressive

## Legal / Document Preparation
Who: Legal admins, document prep services, paralegal services
Primary CTA: Fill out a form / Get a quote
Audience device: Desktop-first (often research-heavy decisions)
Trust signals: Turnaround time, document types handled, 
compliance language, years in operation
Font: Inter + Playfair Display
Design archetype: Premium & Sleek
Hero headline formula: "[Document type] filed correctly — in [X hours/days]"
Tone: Calm, authoritative, reassuring
Tone to avoid: Overly casual, salesy, fear-mongering

## Code Violation Officers / Services
Who: Officers building community trust, or services helping 
property owners resolve violations
Primary CTA: Call / Fill out form
Audience device: Mobile-first
Trust signals: Case resolution rate, types of violations handled, 
local jurisdiction knowledge, fast response
Font: Inter + Space Grotesk
Design archetype: Warm & Trustworthy
Hero headline formula: "Clear your violation before the deadline"
Tone: Direct, empathetic, urgency without panic
Tone to avoid: Intimidating, bureaucratic, cold

## Pressure Washing (Commercial)
Who: Commercial property owners, facility managers, HOAs
Primary CTA: Get a free quote
Audience device: Mobile-first
Trust signals: Before/after photos, property types served, 
insured/licensed badge, response time
Font: Inter + Space Grotesk
Design archetype: Bold & Disruptive
Hero headline formula: "Your [property type] cleaned and documented — 
same week"
Tone: Confident, no-nonsense, results-first
Tone to avoid: Too casual, unprofessional, price-focused

## Commercial Painter / Course Seller
Who: Commercial painter selling a course on entering the trade
Primary CTA: Purchase course / Apply now
Audience device: Mobile-first (social media traffic)
Trust signals: Creator's revenue/jobs completed, student results, 
course curriculum preview, money-back guarantee
Font: DM Sans + Lora
Design archetype: Bold & Disruptive
Hero headline formula: "Become a commercial painter — your first 
contract in 60 days"
Tone: Aspirational, peer-to-peer, honest about the work required
Tone to avoid: Hype-heavy, corporate, overly polished

## Zooted Farms (Streetwear / Creative Brand)
Who: Streetwear consumers, cannabis-adjacent culture, 
creative community
Primary CTA: Shop now / Explore the drop
Audience device: Mobile-first (social media native)
Trust signals: Character design quality, limited drops, 
community identity, cultural authenticity
Font: Syne + Bebas Neue
Design archetype: Creative Brand OVERRIDE
  → Dark editorial palette, no gradient mesh
  → Bold typographic hero, character-forward layout
  → Aggressive motion: parallax, stagger animations
Hero headline formula: Not formula-driven — lead with the 
character/story, not an outcome
Tone: Cultural, energetic, raw, community-first
Tone to avoid: Corporate, generic streetwear clichés, safe

## Pet / Animal Products (Furry Furrows)
Who: Pet owners who treat pets as family, freeze-dried treat buyers
Primary CTA: Shop now / Learn more
Audience device: Mobile-first
Trust signals: Ingredients transparency, sourcing story, 
pet owner testimonials with photos, no filler badge
Font: Nunito + Merriweather
Design archetype: Warm & Trustworthy
Hero headline formula: "Real ingredients. Real treats. 
[Pet name]-approved."
Tone: Warm, playful but credible, parent-to-parent
Tone to avoid: Clinical, cold, overly scientific
