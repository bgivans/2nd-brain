## Credit Rebuild Plan

- **Current:** ~630–640
- **Target:** 700+ (required for good loan terms on a SoCal purchase)
- **Timeline:** 12-18 months of consistent positive activity to see meaningful movement

**From April–May 2025 credit sessions (25+ message deep dive):**

The path from 630→700+ requires:
1. **No new negative marks** — pay everything on time, no exceptions
2. **Authorized user on a strong account** — if someone with a 750+ score adds you, their history helps yours
3. **Secured credit card** — $200-500 deposit, use it for one recurring bill, pay in full monthly
4. **Credit utilization below 30%** — on any open revolving credit
5. **Dispute stale negatives** — items older than 7 years may be removable

**The bill payment approach (May 2025 session):**
Adding recurring monthly bills (utilities, subscriptions) to a credit card and paying in full each month creates positive payment history without debt accumulation. The card usage itself builds the score — the key is paying in full so no interest accrues.

**Historical context:** The 630 score hasn't moved despite being tracked since at least March 2025. That means there has been no active credit building — just no new damage. Movement requires action.
