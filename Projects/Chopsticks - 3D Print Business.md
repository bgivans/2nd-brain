# Chopsticks — 3D Print Business

**Partners:** Bryce + [[People/Brielle]]
**Theme:** Kitchen-related 3D printed products (staying on theme with the name)
**Goal:** Sell 1,000 products (physical or digital). Have fun doing it.

---

## Division of Work

**Brielle:**
- Design concepts / ideas
- Making things cute
- Cleaning prints / organizing inventory
- Content / marketing modeling and acting
- Content editing

**Bryce:**
- Design concepts / ideas
- Printing (Ender SE3)
- File management
- Website / storefront / funnel
- Helping shoot content

---

## Business Model

**Sell physical products** AND **sell the design file:**

| Product type | Price |
|---|---|
| Personal use file | $9 |
| Commercial file | $75 |

*Note: not factoring ~15% material costs*

**The math:** 213 digital sales/week = $100K/year (all digital). 321 physical/week to hit same. Target is physical first to prove demand, then shift toward digital.
