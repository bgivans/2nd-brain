# SDSS — Sign Services (Urban Management)

> **Brainstorming context:** Numbers and structures below are ideated projections and models, not confirmed business results.

**Parent:** [[Projects/Urban Management]]
**What it is:** Real estate sign setting + media services sold to agents and brokers. Outbound calling model targeting the ~8,250 agents in San Diego (3,400 offices).

---

## Business Model

Agents buy sign setting services on a recurring basis. Simple, physical product with predictable frequency.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Avg deal size | $120/transaction |
| Average agent buys | 2x every 3 months (~$480/year) |
| Higher spend | $960/year (sign setting alone) |
| San Diego market | ~8,250 agents, 3,400 offices |
| Target list needed | 15,360 leads |

**Tier structure being explored:** signs only → signs + media → full media package

---

## Calling Operation

**Current baseline (Carlos example):**
- 20 calls → 14 no answer → 6 answered → 3 interested
- 1.5 hrs calling + 45 min follow-up emails/texts + 15 min Bryce pulling/organizing data
- Carlos closes ~1 deal per session at $120

**Projected at scale (1 full-time TJ caller):**
- 40 hrs/week
- 16 new clients/week
- 768 new clients/year
- ~$1.1M/year gross at avg $960 spend

**TJ caller cost model:**
- Salary: $700/month (~$28,967 peso)
- Commission: 10% per closed deal ($12 on $120 sale)
- 3 sales/day = $720/month commission
- Total cost: ~$1,420/month
- Net to SDSS: ~$6,480/month
- **550% ROI on labor cost**

## Dialer Workflow (Designed by Bryce)

1. CSV upload with URL embedded → auto-populates in Wave Dialer
2. Drop-down disposition → routes call to correct opportunity tab in GHL
3. Immediate thank-you text after call
4. Auto text follow-up at 2 weeks, 2 months, 6 months (dropdown)
5. Auto calendar notification to call back on set date
6. Color-coded goals (green = hit 100 calls)
7. Callbacks routed to mobile app

**Calling stats:**
- 34 seconds/call average
- 17% connect rate
- 20 part-time callers × 20 hrs = 165,600 dialed → 28,000 answered → 420 appointments → 60 closed (1/7 close rate) = ~$5M
