## What Hippotanicals Is

Eco-friendly, vegan dental and body care brand. Founded 2020 after Dr. Ann developed a severe allergic reaction and couldn't find clean, natural alternatives.

**Products:**
- Dental: bamboo toothbrushes, natural toothpaste tablets, vegan floss
- Body: lip balms, soaps, lotion bars, shampoo bars, conditioner bars
- **Flagship:** "Total Oral Care" subscription box — $54.00 every 3 months

**Mission:** Plant-based, allergen-free personal care with minimal ingredients, biodegradable packaging.

## Operational Bottlenecks (Identified)

1. **Manual packaging & shipping** — Dr. Ann does this herself, major time drain
2. **Inventory data entry** — lots of manual updating, no automation
3. **Manufacturing minimums** — 100K+ units per SKU through manufacturers; small-batch solution being explored (renting a local kitchen + day workers)
   - Caution: big retailers (Target, CVS) require spore testing + other compliance — small kitchen likely only viable for boutiques
4. **Buyback risk** — if products don't sell off shelves, stores can request buyback. Products have ~1-year shelf life
5. **Faire.com constraint** — can't proactively contact Faire buyers; they must initiate

## Ops Manager / COO Job Outline (Built for Mimi)

### Role
Operations Manager → potential growth path to COO
Part-time to start, transitions full-time as revenue grows
Reports to Dr. Ann

### Key Responsibilities

**Supply Chain & Fulfillment**
- Oversee packaging, shipping, order fulfillment
- Manage manufacturer relationships (100K+ SKU minimums)
- Explore small-batch production alternatives (local kitchen)
- Automate/streamline inventory tracking and data entry

**Vendor & Store Relationship Management** *(Primary KPI: vendor retention)*
- Nurture relationships with 60+ stores and Faire.com buyers
- Handle cold outreach + follow-up for new store acquisitions
- Refine pitching process to improve ~5% conversion rate
- Hire outreach/email support when volume demands it

**Financial Oversight**
- Maintain minimum 30% product margin on any price adjustments
- Approve partnerships/orders under $10K (above = Dr. Ann approval)
- Business purchases under $500/month without approval
- Monthly financial reporting to Dr. Ann

**Team Management & Hiring**
- First-round hiring filter; Dr. Ann does final interview
- Day-to-day team management as headcount grows

**Marketing Oversight**
- Approve social media content
- Coordinate with social media manager when hired
- Growth strategies for Instagram and Faire.com

**Sales & Growth** *(Primary KPI: revenue growth)*
- Store/vendor outreach — pitch training provided
- Expand into high-end boutiques and eco-conscious retailers
- Track conversion rates and optimize outreach funnel
