## What Furry Furrows Is

Premium freeze-dried dog treats targeting 9-to-5 working dog parents who feel guilty being away from their pets. Core positioning: **the homecoming treat** — a daily reconnection ritual.

**Hero tagline:** *"Homecoming treats for working dog parents"*

**Brand personality:** Playful & luxurious — humor + heart. The "fun" freeze-dried brand in a too-serious market.

**Target customer:**
- 9-to-5ers who feel guilty not being home with their dog
- Taking dogs to beach/dog park on weekends
- Dog parents with older dogs or health-conscious pet care habits
- Sensitive stomach dog breeds
- Dogs with social tendencies (neighborhood walks, dog parks)

## Regulatory Snapshot (California)

- **State:** CA Pet Food Processor Registration required even for packaging/labeling/warehousing activity
- **Labeling (AAFCO):** Guaranteed analysis required — min crude protein/fat, max crude fiber/moisture
- **Health claims:** Cannot make illegal health claims on label
- **Risk:** Some states don't allow cottage-style home production with freeze-drying — baked treats only
- **Compliance cost:** Product liability insurance starts ~$299/year. Micro testing per lot: Salmonella $52, Listeria $52

**Hidden risk to flag to Byron:** Sanitation SOPs, lot traceability, and recordkeeping requirements hit hard at small scale.
