## Anastasia — @girlinbluestudios

**Why she's in here:** Not the aesthetics — the sequencing. Emotional brief → utility-first naming → cohesive campaign. Name, product, and marketing all point at the same thing.

**Key observation:** Her punny business names work because they're accurate first and clever second. The cleverness is earned.

**Content format worth noting:** "If I were to create a [X] brand" — demonstrates methodology publicly without exposing client work. Worth considering for own content.
